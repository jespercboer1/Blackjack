Objective

The goal is to beat the dealer by getting a hand value closer to 21 than the dealer’s hand, without exceeding 21. If your total goes over 21, you bust and automatically lose the round.

Card Values

  • Number cards (2–10): Face value
  • Jack, Queen, King: 10 points
  • Ace: 1 or 11 (11, until your hand value is over 21)

Game Flow

Each round:

  • You and the dealer are dealt two cards.
  • One of the dealer’s cards remains hidden until the end of the round.
  • You choose actions until you stand, bust, or reach 21.
  • The dealer then plays according to fixed rules.
  • The winner is determined and the round ends.

After each round, the deck is reshuffled and a new round begins.

Dealer Rules

  • The dealer draws cards until reaching at least 17.
  • A configurable rule determines behavior on a soft 17:
    • Soft 17 = Hit or Stand (toggle setting)

Player Actions

Hit

Draw another card to increase your total.

Stand

Keep your current hand and end your turn.

Double Down

  • You may double your bet at any time (no restrictions on hand value).
  • You receive exactly one additional card and then automatically stand.
  • Double is allowed after splitting hands.

Split

  • Allowed once per round when the first two cards have the same value.
  • Your hand is split into two separate hands.
  • Each hand is played independently.
  • Splitting aces is allowed.

Surrender

  • You may surrender at any time before finishing your turn.
  • You forfeit your full bet and end the round immediately.

Blackjack (Natural 21)

  • A blackjack occurs when the first two cards total 21 (Ace + 10-value card).
  • Blackjack pays 6:5.
  • If both player and dealer have blackjack, the result is a tie.

Dealer Blackjack

If the dealer also has a blackjack:

  • Player blackjack results in a tie
  • Otherwise, the player loses

Winning Conditions

You win if:

  • Your total is higher than the dealer’s without busting
  • The dealer busts (exceeds 21)
  • You have blackjack and the dealer does not

You lose if:

  • You bust
  • The dealer has a higher valid hand

You tie if:

  • Both hands have equal value
  • Both have blackjack

Deck Rules

  • Standard 52-card deck is used.
  • No duplicate cards exist within a single round.
  • After each round (win, loss, or tie), the deck is fully reshuffled.

Core gameplay rules are designed to remain consistent across updates.
