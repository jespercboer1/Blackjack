Blackjack game flow:
    1. Place your bet.
    2. Cards are dealt:
        You get 2 cards.
        The dealer gets 2 cards (1 face up, 1 face down(depending on difficulty)).
    3. Check for blackjack:
        If you have 21 with your first 2 cards, you have blackjack.
    4. Your turn:
        Hit → Take another card.
        Stand → Keep your hand.
        (Optional in many games: Double Down, Split, Surrender.)
    5. Dealer's turn:
        Reveal the hidden card.
        Draw until reaching at least 17 (following casino rules).
    6. Determine the winner:
        If you go over 21, you lose.
        If the dealer goes over 21, you win.
        Otherwise, the hand closest to 21 wins.
        Equal totals = push (tie, your bet is returned).
